To create an event, log in to your Festiva account and click on "Create Event" in your dashboard. Follow the step-by-step wizard to add event details, tickets, and settings. You can publish your event immediately or save it as a draft to finish later.

Festiva accepts all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. For event organizers, we support payouts via direct bank transfer or PayPal.

Yes, most tickets can be transferred to another person. To transfer a ticket:

  1. Log in to your Festiva account
  2. Go to "My Tickets"
  3. Select the ticket you want to transfer
  4. Click "Transfer Ticket" and enter the recipient's details

Some event organizers may disable this feature for their events.

You can contact event organizers directly through the event page:

  1. Navigate to the event page
  2. Scroll to the "Organizer" section
  3. Click "Contact Organizer"
  4. Fill out the contact form with your message

Organizers typically respond within 24-48 hours.
